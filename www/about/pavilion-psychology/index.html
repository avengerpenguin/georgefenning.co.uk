Pavilion Psychology



This is the trading name of the expanded independent psychological service run by George Fenning Consultant Psychologist who has been delivering a comprehensive range of treatment and consultancy services since 1989.

Treatment

  • Treatment services can be provided in many locations to suit the client and/or organisation.
  • Referrals are accepted from a wide variety of sources GPs, solicitors, psychiatrists, physicians, agencies, occupational health doctors etc
  • George Fenning is a registered provider with major health insurance companies.

Consultancy

  • The Consultancy Service accepts referrals from a wide variety of professionals, healthcare organisations and academic establishments.

 

PSYMET - a specialised CLINICAL OUTCOME package for existing treatment services involving data collection and training

Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) – Pavilion Psychology provides a specialised services for victims of trauma
