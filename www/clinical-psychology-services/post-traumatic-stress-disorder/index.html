PTSD – Trauma

Pavilion Psychology specialises in the treatment of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Services are designed treat victims of common problems such as :-

  • Road Traffic Accidents
  • Industrial Injuries and Work Trauma
  • Physical, Emotional and Sexual Abuse
  • Assault

Clients are usually offered a combination of :-

  • Trauma Focused Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (TF-CBT)
  • EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing)
  • Family and relationship /sex therapy for affected individuals
  • General CBT for problems with associated guilt and anger
