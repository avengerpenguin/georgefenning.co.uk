Therapies

TREATMENT BROCHURE 2013


Pavilion Psychology offers a range of Evidence Based Treatments as recommended by the National Institute for Health and Clinical Excellence (NICE) and these include:

  • Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT) used for a wide range of conditions such as anxiety and depression including Obsessive Compulsive Disorder and Generalised Anxiety Disorder
  • Trauma Focused Cognitve Behaviour Therapy (TF-CBT) is used in treating Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and other Stress Reactions. This is often used in conjunction with EMDR below
  • Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing (EMDR) This useful adjunct to therapy is offered as part of a more complex Trauma Focused Cognitive Behaviour Therapy treatment package in cases of PTSD
  • Behaviour Couples Therapy (BCT) along with CBT above, this is often used in helping people with addictions who have the support of a spouse or partner who wish to play a part in the overall therapy treatment plan.
All of the above have a good research evidence base associated with clinical effectiveness and positive therapeutic outcomes in a range of common problems.
